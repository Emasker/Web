形状函数

The moon is the fifth largest satellite in the solar system, with an average radius of about 1737.10 kilometers, equivalent to 0.273 times the radius of the earth; The mass is close to 7.342 × 1022 kg, equivalent to 0.0123 times of the earth . The surface of the moon is covered with craters formed by the impact of small celestial bodies. The average distance between the moon and the earth is about 384400 kilometers, about 30 times the diameter of the earth.
